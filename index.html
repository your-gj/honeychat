What is Honey Gonna Chat?

Honey Gonna Chat (honeygonnachat.pages.dev) is a private, invite-only chat space built for couples. Every couple who signs up gets their own room — completely separate from everyone else's — reachable only through two permanent links, one for each partner. There's no public feed and no way for strangers to find or join a room. Messages clear automatically at midnight for a fresh daily start. Honey Gonna Chat was built by Gautam Jaiswal, founder of YourWeblancer, together with Hemane Rana.

Terms & Conditions

Please read before creating your room.

1. Purpose

Honey Gonna Chat is a private space for two consenting adults in a relationship to communicate. It is not a public platform, dating app, or social network.

2. Age requirement

You must be 18 years or older to create an account or use Honey Gonna Chat in any way.

3. Acceptable use

  • No illegal content or activity of any kind.
  • No nudity, sexually explicit images, or sexual content involving any person.
  • No harassment, threats, or abuse directed at your partner or anyone else.
  • No impersonation, fraud, or misuse of another person's link or account.

4. No end-to-end encryption

Honey Gonna Chat is not end-to-end encrypted. Messages are stored so they can be delivered and are cleared automatically at midnight. Treat the space as private, not as unbreakable.

5. Liability

We are not liable for data leaks, unauthorized access resulting from a shared password or link, or any consequence of content you or your partner choose to send. Use Honey Gonna Chat at your own discretion.

6. Enforcement

Accounts found violating these terms may be suspended or removed without notice.
